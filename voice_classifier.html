Speaker Classification Policy Evolution

Version 4 — Duration Constraint & Production Reality

V4 constrained training to >5s audio after discovering short segments were error-prone. Embedding geometry improved. Separation tightened. But production revealed the deeper issue: High accuracy does not guarantee reliability. The system failed confidently at cluster scale — mislabeling entire speaker groups with certainty. The solution was architectural: Map NeMo speaker_IDs directly to timestamped audio and introduce Human-In-The-Loop verification. Accuracy is a metric. Reliability is a system property.
V4 Key Findings
  • >5s training constraint
  • Improved embedding compactness
  • Confident misclassification at production scale
  • Shift to HITL speaker_ID verification
